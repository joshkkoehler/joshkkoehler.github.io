Abraham Lincoln

A portrait of the 16th president of the United States, Abraham Lincoln.

1809 - Lincoln was born in Kentucky
1818 - Nancy Lincoln, Mother of Abraham dies of milk sickness
1830 - Lincoln and several family members move to Illinois
1842 - Abraham marries Mary Todd who he met in Springfield, Illinois
1846 - Lincoln is elected to the House of Representatives
1860 - Lincoln is elected the 16th President of the United States
1865 - Abraham Lincoln is assassinated at age 56

All information has been sourced from his Wikipedia Article.
